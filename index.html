Welcome to GitHub Pages.

Psychocybernetics, neural networking and virtualization. Built using free and open source software. Features: Full simulation suite for the natural sciences and number theory, including protocols for particle physics and graphical simulation GNU/Linux virtual operating system, combining the best of TRISQUEL and UBUNTU. Copy the master onto a USB stick, plug and play and you're good to go Fight crime on the fly with the GNU privacy guard Build your own interfaces Written in the analytic philosophical tradition, with the best of Wittgenstein, Frege, Russell, Varela, Kauffman, Nietzsche, Spinoza and Captain Jean-Luc Picard Download now and solve your own maze problems today! Built to work especially with your own "mining" tools. Completely modularized so you can pick up right from the top-level directory (but you don't have to).
